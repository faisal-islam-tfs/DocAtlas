Legacy Full Text Workbook Structure
==================================

Workbook name pattern:
- <app_slug>__docatlas_full_text.xlsx

Sheet:
- FullText

Typical columns:
- doc_id, file_key, category, short_summary, long_summary, tags,
  word_count, char_count, extraction_status, review_flags,
  full_text, full_text_parts_count, full_text_part_1..N,
  moved_to, file_name, file_path

Purpose:
- Preserve complete extracted document text split into Excel-safe chunks.

Status:
- Full-text workbook output is currently disabled by default.
- Re-enable by running with --include-full-text-output.
